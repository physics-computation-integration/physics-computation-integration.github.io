The wider community

Ambassadors help computation travel.

Ambassadors are physics educators from additional institutions who join the conversation, offer feedback on the work, and help explore how computation integration can take root in different departments.

What Ambassadors do

Bring other departments' perspectives.

Ambassadors participate in regular conversations about project progress and emerging resources. Their questions and experience help the collaboration make its products useful beyond the core partner departments.

The cohort includes educators working in two-year, four-year, and PhD-granting institutions. We look forward to working with colleagues who are curious about what computation can add to physics education.
